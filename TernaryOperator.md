# Ternary operator

| Operator | Example | Meaning    |
| ------ | ------ |    ------          |
| &      | a & b  |   Bitwise AND |
| ^ |  a ^ b|Bitwise XOR (exclusive OR)|
| ~ | ~a|Bitwise NOT|
| << |  a << n|Bitwise left shift|
| >> | a >> n |	Bitwise right shift|
